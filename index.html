Volodymyr Zelenskyy

Volodymyr Zelenskyy is the president of Ukraine. Before becoming president he was a comedian and an actor on the show Servant of the People.

Early life

He was born on 25th January 1978 in Kryvyi Rih in Ukraine.

Before starting school, he lived in Mongolia for 4 years.

He completed a law degree at Kryvyi_Rih_National_University, but he never got a job related to his degree.

Comedy Career

1997

He entered a comedy competition representing his country and won.

He created the comedy group Kvartal 95. This competed in competitions into 2003.

2003

He started producing shows for Ukrainian TV with Kvartal 95.

2006

Won Dancing with the stars. This is a US dance competition similar to strictly come dancing.

2010-2012

He worked at the TV channel Inter, as a general producer.

2015

Played a history teacher who became the Ukrainian president on TV, on the show Servant of the People.

2018

Political Career

March 2018

Created a new political party with the same name as his last comedy show.

December 2018

Announced he would run for the role of president.

This was done virtually using Youtube and Kvaryal 95.

His policies were to prevent corruption and end the war in Donbas.

March 2019

He won the first round of the elections.

April 2019

He won the election to become president with 73% of the vote.

May 2019

He was inaugurated as president with Volodymyr Groysman as the prime minister.

His first policy was attempting to change the parliamentary system to proportional representation and arrange early parliamentary elections.

July 2019

He cancelled Ukrainian independence day parades, to save money and spend it on veterans.

He held a phone call with Vladimir Putin to exchange prisoners from the war in Donbas.

September 2019

He removed immunity from prosecution from judges, lawmakers and diplomats.

This was one of his main policies during the election.

October 2019

He agreed to a deal with Putin to respect the results of elections in the Russian occupied areas of Donbas, and withdraw its unmarked troops in the area.

This failed to stop Putin from attacking the area.

December 2019

He resumed negotiations with France, Germany and Russia to end the war that had previously ended in 2016.

These were the first negotiations done face to face with Putin.

February 2020

He passed new media laws to decrease the power of Ukrainian Oligarchs.

July 2020

He agreed on a ceasefire with Russia. This was the 20th ceasefire between the two countries. It was broken however, ceasefires were broken 50% less often than in 2019.

January 2021

He reformed Ukrainian laws on referendums.

April 2021

He met with Joe Biden to lobby Nato to accept Ukraine into the EU.

June 2021

A list of all the Ukrainian oligarchs was created and they were banned from buying privatised companies and donating money to political parties.

Invasion of Ukraine

February 2022

Putin intensified the war and aimed to invade the entire country.

Zelenskyy decided to stay in Ukraine and fight. He rejected offers to help him evacuate and said I need ammunition, not a ride.

Martial law was declared in Ukraine. The army was mobilised and men between 18 and 60 were banned from leaving Ukraine.

He was considered a hero throughout the world with an approval of 90% in Ukraine.

He addressed Europe, Canada, the USA, and Japan and asked them to isolate Russia and support Ukraine's army.

March 2022

It was announced that he had survived 3 assassination attempts in a single week. These had been sabotaged by Russian spies who disagreed with the war.

He negotiated with Putin to end the invasion, and he was told he must surrender Donetsk, Luhansk and Crimea, so the war continued.

A deepfake video was released where he told the country to surrender to Russia.

He banned 11 Ukrainian political parties for supporting Russia.

May 2022

Turkey was criticised for allowing Russian tourists into the country, while it simultaneously hosted negotiations to end the war.

The EU was lobbied to extend its sanctions to fossil fuels. It had paid Russia €56 billion in the first 3 months of the war.

He announced he was satisfied with China for its policy of staying neutral.

June 2022

Russia had been making steady gains in the war, and he announced that Russia had seized 20% of Ukraine.
